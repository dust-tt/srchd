## Definitions

Let $\epsilon>0$. Does there exist $A\subseteq \mathbb{N}$
such that the lower density of $A+A$ is at least $1-\epsilon$
and yet $1_A\ast 1_A(n) \ll_\epsilon 1$ for all $n$?
