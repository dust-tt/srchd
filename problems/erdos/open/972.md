## Definitions

The set of primes `p` such that `Nat.floor (α * p)` is also prime.

**Erdős problem 972.**
Let $\alpha > 1$ be irrational. Are there infinitely many primes $p$
such that $\lfloor p\alpha \rfloor$ is also prime?
