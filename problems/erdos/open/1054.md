## Definitions

Let $f(n)$ be the minimal integer $m$ such that $n$ is the sum of the $k$ smallest
divisors of $m$ for some $k\geq 1$.-/
noncomputable def f (n : ℕ) : ℕ :=
  if h : ∃ᵉ (m) (k ≥ 1), n = ∑ i < k, Nat.nth (· ∈ m.divisors) i then
    Nat.find h
  else 0
