## Definitions

Is there a function $f$ with $f(n)\to\infty$ as $n\to\infty$ such that,
for all large $n$, there is a composite number $m$ such that
$$
n + f(n) < m < n + p(m)
$$
Here $p(m)$ is the least prime factor of $m$.
