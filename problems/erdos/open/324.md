## Definitions

Does there exist a polynomial $f(x)\in\mathbb{Z}[x]$ such that all the sums $f(a)+f(b)$ with
$a < b$ nonnegative integers are distinct?

Probably $f(x) = x^5$ has the property that the sums $f(a)+f(b)$ with
$a < b$ nonnegative integers are distinct.
