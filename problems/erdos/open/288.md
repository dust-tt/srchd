## Definitions

Is it true that there are only finitely many pairs of intervals $I_1$, $I_2$ such that
$$
\sum_{n_1 \in I_1} \frac{1}{n_1} + \sum_{n_2 \in I_2} \frac{1}{n_2} \in \mathbb{N}?
$$
