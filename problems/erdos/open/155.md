## Definitions

Let $F(N)$ be the size of the largest Sidon subset of $\{1, \dots, N\}$.
-/
noncomputable abbrev F (N : ℕ) : ℕ := Finset.maxSidonSubsetCard (Finset.Icc 1 N)

/--
Is it true that for every $k \geq 1$ we have
$$
F(N + k) \leq F(N) + 1
$$
for all sufficiently large $N$?
