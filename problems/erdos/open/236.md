## Definitions

$f(n)$ counts the number of solutions to $n=p+2^k$ for prime $p$ and $k\geq 0$.

Let $f(n)$ count the number of solutions to $n=p+2^k$ for prime $p$ and $k\geq 0$. Show that $f(n)=o(\log n)$.
