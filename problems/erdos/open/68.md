## Definitions

Is
$$\sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{1}{n!-1}$$
irrational?

$$\sum_{n=2}^\infty \frac{1}{n!-1} = \sum_{n=2}^\infty \sum_{k=1}^\infty \frac{1}{(n!)^k}$$
