## Problem Statement

There exists some constant $c&#62;0$ such that

$$R(C_4,K_n) \ll n^{2-c}.$$

## Background

The current bounds are

$ \frac{n^{3/2}}{(\log n)^{3/2}}\ll R(C_4,K_n)\ll \frac{n^2}{(\log n)^2}.$

The upper bound is due to Szemer&#233;di (mentioned in [[EFRS78]](javascript:void(0);)), and the lower bound is due to Spencer [[Sp77]](javascript:void(0);).

This problem is [#17 in Ramsey Theory](https://mathweb.ucsd.edu/~erdosproblems/erdos/newproblems/RamseyC4.html) in the graphs problem collection.
