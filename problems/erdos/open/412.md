## Definitions

Let $σ_1(n)=σ(n)$, the sum of divisors function, and $σ_k(n) = σ(σ_{k-1}(n))$.
Is it true that, for every $m, n ≥ 2$, there exist some $i, j$ such that $σ_i(m) = σ_j(n)$?
