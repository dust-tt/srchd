## Definitions

`IsLPF p m` says that `p` is the least prime factor of `m`.

**Erdős problem 681.**
Is it true that for all large $n$ there exists $k$
such that $n + k$ is composite and $p(n+k) > k^2$,
where $p(m)$ is the least prime factor of $m$ ?
