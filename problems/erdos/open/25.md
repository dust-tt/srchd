## Problem Statement

Let $n_1&#60;n_2&#60;\cdots$ be an arbitrary sequence of integers, each with an associated residue class $a_i\pmod{n_i}$. Let $A$ be the set of integers $n$ such that for every $i$ either $n&#60;n_i$ or $n\not\equiv a_i\pmod{n_i}$. Must the logarithmic density of $A$ exist?
