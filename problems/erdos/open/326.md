## Definitions

Let $A \subset \mathbb{N}$ be an additive basis of order 2.

Must there exist $B = \{b_1 < b_2 < \dots\} \subseteq A$ which is also a basis such that
$\lim_{k\to\infty} \frac{b_k}{k^2}$ does not exist?
