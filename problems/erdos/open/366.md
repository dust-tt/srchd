## Definitions

Are there any $2$-full $n$ such that $n+1$ is $3$-full?

Note that $8$ is $3$-full and $9$ is 2-full.

Are there infinitely many 3-full $n$ such that $n+1$ is 2-full?

Are there any consecutive pairs of $3$-full integers?
