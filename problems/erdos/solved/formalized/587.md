## Problem Statement

`MaxNotSqSum N` is the size of the largest subset `A` of
`{1,...,N}` such that for all non-empty `S ⊆ A`, the sum
`∑ n ∈ S, n` is not a square.

## Variants and Related Results

Nguyen and Vu proved that $|A| \ll N^{1/3} (\log N)^{O(1)}$.
